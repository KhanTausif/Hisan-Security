SERVICES

Security Audit

VAPT & Threat Detection

VAPT involves identifying system weaknesses through testing and actively probing for vulnerabilities.
Threat Detection focuses on monitoring systems for signs of potential cyber threats or malicious activities, aiming to identify and respond to suspicious behavior or security breaches promptly.

Security Policies, Incident Response & Documenting

Security policies and procedures review company protocols for passwords, user access, latest patches .
Vulnerability assessment targets weaknesses in software or systems. Incident response readiness ensures preparedness with response plans and backups.
Documenting involves thoroughly examining digital systems, documenting their security measures, identifying potential risks, and proposing improvements to ensure robust protection against cyber threats.

Compliance and Regulations

To ensure organizations follow specific guidelines to protect data and meet industry standards, preventing legal issues and ensuring trust, adhering compliances like HIPPA, PCI DSS, ISO 27001 & DGDP.

Endpoint Security

Next Generation Antivirus & Patch Management

NGAV utilizes AI, machine learning, and behavior analysis to identify and prevent advanced threats in the endpoints.
Patch Management ensures operating systems, software and databases are regularly updated with latest security patches.

Firewall & Encryption

Monitors and controls incoming/outgoing network traffic to prevent unauthorized access to the endpoint devices.
Protects data on devices and during transmission to prevent unauthorized access.

Device Control & User Authentication

Device control aims to enhance security by managing and securing the diverse range of devices that connect to an organization's network, reducing potential risks and maintaining a secure computing environment.
Verifies user identity to control access to devices and sensitive data.

Data Encryption and Backup

File, Folder and Drive Encryption

File encryption secures individual files, folder encryption extends this to entire folders, and drive encryption protects entire storage devices, all requiring authorized access to decrypt and view the data, ensuring confidentiality and preventing unauthorized access.

Data Backup

Data backup involves creating backups of essential files, folders, or entire systems to safeguard against data loss due to accidental deletion, hardware failures, or cyberattacks

Data Loss Prevention

Policy Enforcement

Implementing rules and policies to control and regulate the handling and movement of sensitive data which includes in emails, clipboard, network sharing.

Endpoint Controls

Applying security measures on devices (endpoints) to prevent data leakage or unauthorized access, such as through external drives or cloud storage.

Zero Trust Architecture Implementation

Identity-Centric & Least Privilege Access Security

Zero Trust revolves around verifying and continuously validating identities before granting access to resources or data.
Users and devices are granted only the minimal level of access needed for their specific tasks, reducing the attack surface.

Continuous Monitoring, Inspection & Micro-Segmentation

All network traffic, including within the network, is continuously monitored for anomalies or potential threats
Network segmentation is implemented at a granular level, dividing the network into smaller segments for better control and security.

MFA & Encryption

Leveraging MFA ensures additional layers of security beyond passwords, reducing the risk of unauthorized access.
Data is encrypted both in transit and at rest, safeguarding it from potential breaches or interceptions.

Continuous Authentication & Visibility Analytics

Users and devices are continuously authenticated throughout their session rather than just at login.
Comprehensive visibility into network traffic and advanced analytics help identify and respond to potential threats swiftly.
